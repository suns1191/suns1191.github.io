Brain Working 24/7 for your growth : Mr. Susshil Moadii

Susshil is a professional RECRUITER having expertise in providing ‘SAP Talent Solutions’ with solid 20 plus years of experience in professional services with an international work exposure in U.S, EMEA, APAC & India.

He started his career as sales professional working in India & eventually moved onto work for partners of ‘Compaq’ range of PC’s “KDCC – Kuwait”.

After returning back to India he joined the ITES industry and got an opportunity to change his career track as a professional recruiter, this is where he learnt the technique of hiring, got the hang of tiny nuances of the recruitment domain, got inclined towards recruitment as a profession and crafted a career roadmap for himself to become a professional recruiter.

The journey began and there was no looking back, he then took up another international opportunity in a Recruitment & Staffing’ firm in “Singapore” as a ‘Recruiter’ and was promoted as ‘Manager – Professional Services” within a quick span of 2 years of his stint with the company.

Excelling further in his career he joined as head of ‘Professional Services’ division of an I.T Service Organisation and consecutively then got an opportunity with two MNC’s, “IBM” and “Credit Suisse” based out of Singapore

Gaining expertise and having mastered the whole gamut of recruitment along with the business knowledge of APAC, EMEA and India in the year June 2007 he decided to be an Entrepreneur by starting a home grown company which is now called “iTek Focal Solutions” based out of Pune City in India providing recruitment solutions focussing only to the I.T companies cutting across technologies and industries.

With detail study of the industry demand, analysis and his own business experience he along with his team have decided to focus on class and not on mass and thus strategically, with a focussed vision has decided to cater ONLY to the SAP ERP suite of Technical & Functional skill sets across all modules in India, APAC & EMEA

Mission : To Provide most holistic recruitment solution with a consultative approach.
